This bar chart provides a visual representation of the number of parks in various states. The X-axis displays the quantity of parks, while the Y-axis represents the corresponding states. This chart allows for a quick comparison of park distribution across different regions, making it easy to identify states with more or fewer parks.

This bar chart illustrates the size in square kilometers of the largest park in various states. The X-axis displays the size values, while the Y-axis represents the respective states. This chart offers a clear visual comparison of the varying park sizes across different regions, enabling you to identify which state boasts the largest natural reserves.
